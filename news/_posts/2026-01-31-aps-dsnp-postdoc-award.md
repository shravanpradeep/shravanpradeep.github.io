Shravan has been selected as a finalist for the American Physical Society's Division of Statistical and Nonlinear Physics (APS DSNP) 2026 Postdoctoral Speaker Award. He will present at the [DSNP Student and Postdoc Speaker Award Session](https://summit.aps.org/smt/2026/events/MAR-C62){:target="_blank"} during the 2026 APS Global Summit, Denver, CO.
